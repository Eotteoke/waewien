Our Story

About Us

Hello, and welcome to Wae Wien Thai Cafe! Here at Wae Wien we have much to offer; from the infamous Thai Green Curry to street food stars like our home-made grilled pork - or 'moo yang'.

We are a family business consisting of Bank, a highly experienced chef not only in Thai cuisine but also in pub food, Japanese cuisine, steak house, and more. The next member: Eang, a house cook since her earliest years of life, and an all-rounded baker specialising in Thai desserts and cakes. Finally, there's me, Bank and Eang's only daughter, the sponge that's absorbed my parents' cooking skills for as long as I can remember.

We are a family that love food and love sharing the warmth that comes with cooking; both literally and figuratively. We believe that relationships are strengthened over a good meal, and so we hope that you, dear customer, will leave Wae Wien with nothing but a smile and a full stomach (maybe a takeaway), and will return for another meal.

Our name, Wae Wien, means 'pop in'. We are overjoyed that on a random day like this, something in you decided to pop into our cafe, and knowing this drives us all to deliver nothing but the best to your table.

Read the menu, take a good look at the pictures, and make sure to try something new. We hope you enjoy!
